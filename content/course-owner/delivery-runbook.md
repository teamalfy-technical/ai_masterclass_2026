# Delivery Runbook

## Contact time
- Total: 8 hours
- Structure: 2 days, 4 hours per day

## Day 1 run order
- 09:00-09:15 Welcome and framing
- 09:15-10:00 The AI map
- 10:00-10:45 How modern AI works
- 10:45-11:00 Break
- 11:00-11:45 Prompting and context engineering
- 11:45-12:30 Codex deep dive
- 12:30-13:00 Codex end-to-end demo

## Day 2 run order
- 09:00-09:15 Recap and reflection
- 09:15-10:00 Building real AI systems
- 10:00-10:45 Codex practical lab
- 10:45-11:00 Break
- 11:00-11:45 OpenClaw architecture and setup
- 11:45-12:30 Skills, ClawHub, and Codex inside OpenClaw
- 12:30-13:00 Capstone workflow workshop

## Demo ratio
- Concepts: 30 percent
- Demos and walkthroughs: 40 percent
- Exercises and discussion: 30 percent

## If time runs short
- Cut secondary examples before cutting the workbook activities.
- Compress Q and A inside modules before cutting the capstone.
- Keep the Codex demo and the final pilot blueprint at all costs.

## Required outputs by the end
- Completed workbook activities
- At least one system map
- One pilot blueprint per team

## Instructor prep checklist
- Demo repository ready
- Codex environment pretested
- OpenClaw walkthrough environment or screenshots ready
- Backup artifacts ready
- Downloads verified
